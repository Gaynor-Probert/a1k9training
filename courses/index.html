Our Dog Training Courses

Dogs love to learn and experience new things and our dog training courses are an excellent way to spend quality time with your canine companion while teaching them new and exciting things.

There is also the added bonus that your dog will make new furry friends and be allowed to play and run off lead with them.

Gaynor Probert offers a range of courses every weekend at A1K9 Behaviour and Training Academy

She holds couses to suit all levels of ability. Gaynor has the specialist knowledge and techniques to help you to overcome any problems you may be have when you train your dog.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Do you train all breeds of dogs?

Answer: Yes we have experience of training almost all breeds of dogs.

Q2: Do we have to book into the Bronze obedience course?

Answer: Yes you do have to book into the Bronze obedience course, as this is a course that runs for six consecutive weeks.

Q3: Can children come to the obedience courses?

Answer: Because the people in the obedience courses are constantly on the move and changing direction, children have to sit on the side and watch. Many do, and they are welcome.

Q4: Do we have to pay in advance when we book into the obedience course?

Answer: You pay for the course on the first week when you arrive at A1K9 Behaviour and Training Academy.

Q5: Where are the courses held?

Answer: They are held at A1K9 Behaviour and Training Academy just outside Pontardulais. (See directions on website)

Q6: Does my dog need to have had kennel cough vaccination to come to classes?

Answer: No your dog does not need to have kennel cough vaccination to come to training classes, but their yearly vaccinations need to be up to date.

Q7: What do I need to bring with me?

Answer: Collar, lead and some treats that your dogs like to eat. We also sell good quality training equipment.

Q8: What should I wear?

Answer: You should wear warm clothes and sturdy shoes as we will work outdoors whenever possible.

Q9: If I miss a week in a course can I have that credited on the next course?

Answer: Sorry no you cannot, as the courses run in six consecutive weeks and you have paid for those dates.

Q10: If it is raining will the class be cancelled?

Answer: No it will be held indoors, in the training barn.
