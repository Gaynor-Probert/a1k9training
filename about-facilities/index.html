A1K9 Facilities

A1K9 Behaviour and Training Academy is set in the heart of the countryside, but is within easy reach, as we are just 5 minutes off junction 47 of the M4.

Gaynor Probert offers a range of courses every weekend at A1K9 Behaviour and Training Academy

She holds couses to suit all levels of ability. Gaynor has the specialist knowledge and techniques to help you to overcome any problems you may be have when you train your dog.

Location

We are located in the Valley of Cwmdulais, just outside Pontardulais, Swansea. It is sometimes called Rhododendron valley and it is at its most beautiful in the spring when all the rhododendrons are in full bloom.

Training Fields

Our training fields are extensive and they all offer panoramic views of the surrounding countryside. We have a wide variety of agility equipment for all sizes of dogs that is great fun for the owner to use with their dog.

Parking

We have ample parking areas for many cars.

Indoor Training

We have a large training barn to enable the training to continue when the weather is inclement, for safety the floor is covered with sand for a soft landing when the dogs are playing.

Shop

We also have a shop that sells high quality training equipment such as leather training leads and collars, buying here ensures that you are trained correctly by experts how to use the equipment.

Hospitality Cabin

We have a log cabin that is a hospitality area, and we are able to sit in comfort and warmth when having a discussion about your dog.
